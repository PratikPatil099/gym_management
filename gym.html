THE FITNESS PROGRAMMES AT GYM

Strength training

Join our Strength Training class to build muscle, increase strength, and enhance overall fitness in a supportive environment. Suitable for all fitness levels!

Yoga

Join our Yoga class to improve flexibility, strength, and mental well-being in a peaceful and welcoming environment. Suitable for all skill levels!

Zumba

Join our Zumba class for a fun and energetic workout that combines dance and fitness! Perfect for all fitness levels, Zumba is a great way to stay active while having a blast.

Cardio heat

A high-energy, fat-burning workout designed to improve endurance and cardiovascular health. Perfect for all fitness levels!

Kickboxing

Kickboxing combines martial arts techniques with high-intensity cardio to create a fun and powerful full-body workout. It’s great for anyone, regardless of fitness level.

Balance training

Improve your stability, coordination, and core strength with our Balance Training class. This low-impact workout is designed for all fitness levels to enhance overall body control.
